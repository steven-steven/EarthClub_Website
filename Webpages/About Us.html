History
On March 10th 2012, Adam was inspired by the Young Master Program, Leadership Conference in JIS, and decided to measure the Carbon Footprint in our school. After measuring the Carbon Footprint in our school, Mr Rick encourages Adam to set goals to reduce the Carbon Footprint in the school. The community and service club focus on environment was born. The first members are Adam and Jae Jun and at that time it was called Carbon Footprint Investigation, and in August it had transformed from a carbon footprint project into an formal after school community and service club. The name had changed into "Earth Club" and it's aim is to make our school more environmental friendly.

Visions:

  • Address Millenium Goals No.7 : Ensure Environmental Sustinability
  • To help improve the environment in the school community and beyond it.
  • To raise awareness for taking care of the environment in our school community.
