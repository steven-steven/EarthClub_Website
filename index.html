Introduction To Earth Club

Earth Club is a Club in Sinarmas Academy where we try to help the environment around us. We strive to bring awareness as well as action to create a sustainable environment in school, the community around it, and the world as a whole. A lot of projectrs had been done in the past, such as recycling, composting, Clean Up Jakarta, and Earth day performances in school and outside school.
